About me

Hi there! My name is Mandorla (which means 'Almond' in Italian!) and I'm a joyful Labrador Retriever
I was born on October 20th, 2022, in a cozy little town in beautiful Sardinia, Italy. Maybe that’s why I’m such a sun-loving, energetic girl!
My life is simple: I adore the sea, and any day spent running on the beach is the best day. My favorite things in the world are playtime, getting all the cuddles I can, and hanging out with my humans. I’m especially fond of children—they make the best playmates and cuddle buddies! They say I have a smile that can melt any heart, and my honey-coloured coat means I always stand out in a crowd.

I'm always ready for a new adventure, especially if treats are involved!
