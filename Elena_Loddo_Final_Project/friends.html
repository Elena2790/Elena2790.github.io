My Friends

Nymeria

Hi, I’m Nymeria, an eight-year-old beagle with a nose that never stops working. I spend my days sniffing every corner of the world—typical beagle business. I may be small, but my curiosity is huge, and I follow every scent like it’s a treasure. After my adventures, I love curling up close to the humans I adore. I’m playful, affectionate, and always ready for my next sniffing mission.

Free

Hi, I’m Frida, but everyone calls me Free, a tiny but energetic chihuahua. I may be small, but I’ve got a big heart and a personality that stands out. I love staying close to my humans—chihuahuas are real “shadow dogs.” I adore cuddles, warm spots, and reminding everyone that even if I’m tiny, I’m definitely the boss.

Jana

Hi, I’m Jana, a white Maremmano sheepdog with a calm but protective spirit. I was born to guard, and I take my job seriously—I’m always watching over the people I love. I’m gentle and affectionate, but also strong and independent. I enjoy quiet moments, wide open spaces, and feeling the wind in my fur. Loyalty is in my nature.
